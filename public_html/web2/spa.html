Rory In Early 20s

540k monthly listeners

Rory In Early 20s is a female Ukrainian producer who now lives in Japan. Her name was based on Rory Mercury from the anime Gate or whatever. Rory specializes in glitchbreak and ambient electronic music. Fun fact: she was the first artist that got me into electronic breakcore stuff!!! Which is why she's my favorite. And not to flex but.. heh... I'm the second place listener globally for her on lastfm... Anyway, a lot of her music contains samples from anime yet there is no chance in hell that you'll know what they are since she takes samples from the most underground things ever. She prefers to make singles instead of albums, but she HAS in fact made albums in the past. Rory releases music on the 7th day of every month, be sure not to miss it!!!!

Goreshit

300k monthly listeners

Goreshit (Leon Makepeace) is an electronic producer from the UK. If you think his stage name is weird, his song names are even WORSE. This guy is the edgiest producer ever, and he makes a lot of songs with shock value titles. But that's just because of his music genre, which is supposed to be so revolting that the evil normies won't listen to it... But I think they're super weird too, so maybe I'm one of the normal people he hates so much. He ALSO uses anime samples from anime such as Lucky Star, Higurashi, Bludgeoning Angel Dokuro Chan, and more. Other than that, Goreshit is a really chill guy. He probably smells bad though.

Machine Girl

1M monthly listeners

Machine Girl was founded in 2012 by Matt Stephenson and later became a duo in 2015 when Sean Kelly joined to play live drums. The duo has been very influencial on the electropunk scene, being very very iconic and well respected. I don't listen to them AS MUCH as the other two, so I don't know too much information, but I do know that they like the movie Ginger Snaps! They've sampled it a bit, so. They also made the soundtrack for the speedrun shooter game Neon White, which I WILL play when I get enough money to, mark my words.
